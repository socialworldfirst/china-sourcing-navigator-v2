Baby product manufacturing in China

China dominates baby product supply chains at every level. 97% of baby carriages imported into the United States come from China, along with 99% of child safety seats and 93% of infant shoes. In 2024, China exported 837 million baby garments worth US$2.2 billion — 77% of all baby clothing exported from Asia. The domestic baby product manufacturing industry is valued at US$181 billion.

What makes China's baby product supply chain different from competitors like Vietnam or Bangladesh is vertical integration and cluster specialisation. Pingxiang County in Hebei produces 145 million strollers, bicycles, and ride-on toys a year — 40% of global output from a single county. Kunshan hosts Goodbaby International, the world's largest stroller company (HK$8.77 billion revenue in 2024). Ningbo clusters car seats, feeding products, and playpens. Guangzhou's Zhongshanba Road has eight wholesale markets dedicated to children's clothing. Each city owns a different part of the baby product supply chain.

Children’s vehicle capital — 145 million strollers, bikes, and ride-ons a year, 40% of global output

Pingxiang County produces 145 million children’s vehicles annually — strollers, baby bicycles, and electric ride-on toys — roughly 40% of global output. The county has 4,800+ manufacturers employing 120,000+ workers across 200+ villages, generating over 30 billion yuan (~US$4.2 billion) in annual revenue. Products export to 100+ countries through retailers including Walmart and Amazon.

Baby strollers and prams are Pingxiang’s second-largest category after children’s bicycles. Factories produce everything from budget umbrella strollers (US$8–15 FOB) to premium travel systems with car seat compatibility (US$40–80 FOB). The newest generation includes smart features — GPS tracking, app-controlled folding, and weight sensors. For export-certified products, verify the factory holds EN 1888 (EU), ASTM F833 (US), or AS/NZS 2088 (Australia). Not all Pingxiang factories can certify for Western markets.

Car seats, feeding products, playpens, and high chairs — Zhejiang’s baby product export hub

Ningbo has one of the densest clusters of baby product manufacturers in China, spanning car seats, strollers, playpens, high chairs, baby walkers, feeding bottles, and baby monitors. Ningbo Baby First Baby Products Co., founded in 1998, holds ECE R44/04, ECE R129, FMVSS213, and OEKO-TEX certifications. ForBaby (Cixi City) has 20 years’ experience specialising in lightweight, affordable strollers for Europe, the Middle East, and North America.

The feeding product cluster includes Ningbo Beierxin Baby Accessories (bottle nipples and nursing products with Japanese-import manufacturing equipment) and multiple silicone product manufacturers producing BPA-free bottles, teethers, and pacifiers to FDA and LFGB standards. Ningbo-Zhoushan Port — the world’s busiest by cargo tonnage — sits 30 minutes from most factories, keeping FOB costs competitive.

Children’s clothing capital — eight wholesale markets on Zhongshanba Road, plus feeding product factories

Guangzhou’s Zhongshanba Road is China’s largest children’s clothing wholesale district. Eight dedicated market buildings — led by Zhong Ba Children’s Garment Plaza (established 1995, 60,000 square metres) — sell baby clothing, children’s wear, and maternity products. The nearby Zhongda Fabric Market supplies raw materials to hundreds of baby clothing manufacturers within the city.

Beyond clothing, Guangzhou hosts feeding product manufacturers like Liben Plastic Industry Co. (baby bottles, nipples, water bottles, established 2006) with injection moulding, blow moulding, and silicone moulding capabilities. Canton Fair Phase 3 (May 1–5 and Oct 31–Nov 4) has a dedicated Area D for baby and maternity products, strollers, cribs, and baby furniture. For buyers combining baby clothing with feeding or nursery products, Guangzhou offers the widest one-city selection.

Goodbaby International HQ — world’s largest stroller company, HK$8.77 billion revenue

Kunshan hosts Goodbaby International (HKEX: 1086), the world’s largest children’s product company. Founded in 1989 with a ‘push and rock’ stroller patent, Goodbaby’s Kunshan campus is the world’s largest stroller factory, producing 10,000+ units daily across 15+ brands including Cybex (HK$4.47 billion revenue in 2024, up 20.8%), Evenflo (HK$2.39 billion), GB, CBX, Rollplay, Happy Dino, and Urbini.

Goodbaby has been the number-one stroller supplier in North America since 1999 and the number-one children’s products brand in China. The company operates seven factories in China plus facilities in the US, Germany, and Mexico. For buyers who need premium-tier strollers with global compliance (ECE R44, FMVSS213, EN 1888), Kunshan is the reference point. Goodbaby does not take small OEM orders, but understanding their quality benchmark helps when evaluating other Chinese suppliers.

Stroller assembly, maternity activewear, and contract manufacturing for international baby brands

Dongguan’s baby product cluster grew from the same Hong Kong and Taiwanese investment that built its toy and electronics industries. Seebaby (Dongguan Chuanghua Industrial Co.) operates a 30,000-square-metre factory producing strollers, walkers, and ride-on toys. Haoyi Baby Products specialises in stroller accessories. Baby-Plus has run stroller and pet stroller sewing and assembly since 2002.

The maternity product side is notable too. Shuyu Activewear produces custom maternity activewear, nursing-friendly fitness tops, and maternity biker shorts from Dongguan. The city’s strength is contract manufacturing with strong quality control infrastructure — ideal for international brands placing OEM/ODM orders with strict specifications.

Also important: Zhili, Huzhou (Zhejiang)

Zhili town in Huzhou is China's children's clothing capital — 14,000+ enterprises producing 2 billion garments annually, accounting for two-thirds of China's children's clothing output. The Zhili China Children's Garment Town covers 700,000 square metres with 3,500+ exhibitors and 40,000 product categories. Nearly 1,000 companies export to 100+ countries. Zhili is not in the Navigator's 20-city list but is a major sourcing destination for baby and children's clothing buyers.

How to source baby products from China

Four ways to find baby product suppliers. Most successful importers use multiple approaches at different stages.

Search on 1688

1688 is Alibaba's domestic B2B marketplace — where Chinese factories trade with each other. For baby products, that means factory-gate pricing on strollers, baby clothing, feeding products, and maternity goods that Alibaba.com doesn't surface. Filter by 平乡 (Pingxiang) for strollers or 宁波 (Ningbo) for feeding products.

How to find baby product suppliers on 1688

1
Search in Chinese

Use Chinese product terms like 婴儿用品 (baby products), 婴儿推车厂家 (baby stroller factory), 童装批发 (children’s clothing wholesale), or 奶瓶厂家 (baby bottle factory).

2
Filter by baby product hubs

Set location to 平乡 (Pingxiang) for strollers and ride-ons, 宁波 (Ningbo) for car seats and feeding, 广州 (Guangzhou) for baby clothing, or 昆山 (Kunshan) for premium strollers.

3
Check factory badges

Look for 超级工厂 (Super Factory) and 实力商家 (Power Merchant). For baby products, safety certifications matter most — check if they list EN 1888, CPSIA, or FDA in their shop description.

4
Verify before paying

Cross-check the business registration on Tianyancha. Baby products carry higher liability risk — verify safety test reports before any order. Use WorldFirst to pay in RMB directly to the supplier’s 1688/Alipay account.

Four ways to find baby product suppliers. Most successful importers use multiple approaches at different stages.

Search on 1688

1688 is Alibaba's domestic B2B marketplace — where Chinese factories trade with each other. For baby products, that means factory-gate pricing on strollers, baby clothing, feeding products, and maternity goods that Alibaba.com doesn't surface. Filter by 平乡 (Pingxiang) for strollers or 宁波 (Ningbo) for feeding products.

How to find baby product suppliers on 1688

1
Search in Chinese

Use Chinese product terms like 婴儿用品 (baby products), 婴儿推车厂家 (baby stroller factory), 童装批发 (children’s clothing wholesale), or 奶瓶厂家 (baby bottle factory).

2
Filter by baby product hubs

Set location to 平乡 (Pingxiang) for strollers and ride-ons, 宁波 (Ningbo) for car seats and feeding, 广州 (Guangzhou) for baby clothing, or 昆山 (Kunshan) for premium strollers.

3
Check factory badges

Look for 超级工厂 (Super Factory) and 实力商家 (Power Merchant). For baby products, safety certifications matter most — check if they list EN 1888, CPSIA, or FDA in their shop description.

4
Verify before paying

Cross-check the business registration on Tianyancha. Baby products carry higher liability risk — verify safety test reports before any order. Use WorldFirst to pay in RMB directly to the supplier’s 1688/Alipay account.
