Outdoor & camping gear manufacturing in China

China produces 48% of the world's camping equipment by volume — more than six times the output of South Korea, the second-largest producer. In 2024, Chinese factories turned out 1.3 million tonnes of camping goods valued at US$5.8 billion, with 904,000 tonnes exported (US$4 billion). Tents alone accounted for 620,000 tonnes of output and US$2 billion in exports — primarily to the United States.

What sets China's outdoor supply chain apart is regional specialisation. Ningbo is the most complete outdoor equipment hub — home to Naturehike and dozens of tent, camping furniture, and sleeping bag manufacturers with direct access to the world's busiest port. Yiwu's Futian Market covers every camping accessory at MOQs as low as 10 pieces. Foshan is the outdoor furniture capital — over 7,000 factories producing rattan, aluminium, and patio sets for gardens worldwide. Over 200 million Chinese consumers now participate in camping domestically, fuelling a 30% annual revenue spike for outdoor gear retailers.

ningbo

China’s most complete outdoor equipment hub — Naturehike HQ, 20+ tent and camping furniture factories, world’s busiest port

Ningbo is the undisputed centre of China’s outdoor equipment export industry. The city hosts the highest concentration of tent, camping furniture, sleeping bag, and outdoor accessory manufacturers anywhere in the country. Naturehike — China’s best-known international outdoor brand, exporting ultralight tents and camping gear to 50+ countries — is headquartered here. DUFFIN has over 22 years supplying camping tents, sleeping bags, camping furniture, and kitchens to Europe, the United States, Canada, Australia, Japan, and Korea.

Pinyi Outdoor Technology (established 2007) is one of China’s most professional outdoor product manufacturers, covering camping furniture, trekking poles, tents, lanterns, chairs, tables, and outdoor kitchens. ETDZ Holdings (established 1986) was one of the first batch export companies in Ningbo and has been ranked among China’s 500 largest import/export enterprises. Ningbo-Zhoushan Port — the world’s busiest by cargo tonnage — sits minutes from most factories, giving outdoor equipment buyers unmatched logistics speed for bulky items like tents and furniture.

yiwu

World’s largest small commodities market — every camping accessory from 10 pieces, ideal for mixed containers

Yiwu International Trade City is the world’s largest wholesale market, and District 2 of the Futian Market is the go-to floor for outdoor and camping products. The range is comprehensive: camping stools, portable picnic tables, camping lamps, folding chairs, picnic mats, camping coffee cups, tents, sleeping bags, and hammocks. Stock items start from 10–100 pieces — making Yiwu the best option for buyers who want to test multiple product lines without committing to factory MOQs.

Key suppliers include Shaying Outdoor Camping Equipment, BaiQi Outdoor Products (tents, relief tents, modular tents), and Cuniu Camping Products (sleeping bags, self-inflating mats, tents). Yiwu’s strength is variety and flexibility: a single buyer can fill a mixed container with camping accessories, outdoor cooking gear, portable furniture, and lighting from different stalls in the same building. Pricing is competitive and negotiation is expected — visit in the slow season (December–February) for the best deals.

foshan

China’s outdoor furniture capital — 7,000+ factories, rattan and aluminium patio sets exported to 100+ countries

Foshan is the global capital of outdoor furniture manufacturing. Shunde District alone hosts thousands of factories specialising in rattan, aluminium, rope-woven, and teak outdoor furniture — dining sets, sofas, loungers, daybeds, and swing chairs. Foshan’s furniture industry achieved an annual output value exceeding US$14.3 billion in 2024, with over 30,000 enterprises (7,000 manufacturers and 20,000 distributors).

Notable outdoor furniture manufacturers include Hongkai Furniture (21 years of expertise, exports to 100+ countries), Shunde Ciao Furniture (outdoor sofas, tables, chairs, loungers, daybeds), Zhechengju Furniture (15 years of outdoor furniture production), and Tai Long Furniture (10,000+ sqm factory, SGS tested). Lecong hosts the world’s largest furniture wholesale market where buyers can see thousands of outdoor sets in a single visit. For buyers sourcing garden and patio furniture alongside camping gear, Foshan is an essential stop.

xiamen

Outdoor bags, roof-top tents, and carbon fibre products — plus Asia’s largest sporting goods trade show

Xiamen is best known as a fitness equipment hub, but it also hosts a significant outdoor products cluster. Yuangu Outdoor Gear manufactures roof-top tents. Forwin Outdoor & Sports Products (established 2005) specialises in ski bags, hydration bags, and waterproof dry bags. Anweisnow Outdoor Products produces hiking backpacks, travel bags, and outdoor gear bags. Esse Outdoor runs a multicultural team developing and testing camping gear across China.

Xiamen’s port provides direct export access, and the city hosts the annual China Sport Show (CSS) — Asia’s largest sporting goods trade event with 1,600+ exhibitors across 160,000+ square metres. The broader Fujian region also contributes outdoor bags and packs, with factories in both Xiamen and Fuzhou producing camping backpacks with strong, waterproof materials. Buyers attending CSS in May can combine supplier meetings with the trade show visit.

guangzhou

Outdoor clothing and bags hub — hammock tents, canopies, quick-drying gear, plus Canton Fair access

Guangzhou is China’s main production hub for outdoor clothing, bags, and suitcases. Quick-drying clothes and outdoor skinsuits are particularly popular with export buyers for their quality-to-price ratio. Traveler Outdoor Equipment specialises in R&D and production of differentiated outdoor equipment including hammock tents, canopies, and peripheral accessories. Xinshui Outdoor Products manufactures tents, folding wagons, and outdoor lights.

Outdoor Kantry Products supplies sleeping bags and camping tents from Guangzhou, while Canis Latrans specialises in outdoor equipment for camping and mountaineering. The Canton Fair (Phase 3, Area D) features approximately 400 camping and outdoor sports suppliers each session — the spring edition runs 1–5 May 2026. Guangzhou is also a strong option for buyers sourcing outdoor apparel alongside camping equipment.

How to source outdoor gear from China

Four ways to find outdoor equipment suppliers. Most successful importers use multiple approaches at different stages.

Search on 1688

1688 is Alibaba's domestic B2B marketplace — where Chinese factories trade with each other. For outdoor gear, that means factory-gate pricing on tents, camping furniture, sleeping bags, and outdoor accessories that Alibaba.com doesn't surface. Filter by 宁波 (Ningbo) for tents or 佛山 (Foshan) for outdoor furniture.

How to find outdoor gear suppliers on 1688

1
Search in Chinese

Use Chinese product terms like 户外用品 (outdoor products), 帐篷厂家 (tent factory), 露营装备批发 (camping equipment wholesale), or 户外家具 (outdoor furniture).

2
Filter by outdoor gear hubs

Set location to 宁波 (Ningbo) for tents and camping furniture, 义乌 (Yiwu) for accessories, 佛山 (Foshan) for outdoor furniture, or 广州 (Guangzhou) for outdoor clothing and bags.

3
Check factory badges

Look for 超级工厂 (Super Factory) and 实力商家 (Power Merchant). For tents, ISO 9001 and CE certification mentions in the shop description indicate export-ready factories.

4
Verify before paying

Cross-check the business registration on Tianyancha. Request waterproof test reports for tents. Use WorldFirst to pay in RMB directly to the supplier’s 1688/Alipay account.
