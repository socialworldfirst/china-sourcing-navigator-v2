What Guangzhou makes

Guangzhou is the gateway to the Pearl River Delta manufacturing belt. The city specialises in apparel, leather goods, cosmetics, toys, and consumer electronics accessories.

How to Find Suppliers in Guangzhou

Search in Chinese

Use the product name in Chinese. WorldFirst’s AI Sourcing Agent translates for you.

3
Check factory badges

Look for 实力商家 (verified merchant) and 源头工厂 (source factory) tags.
