Sourcing from Shenzhen

China's hardware capital. Home to Huaqiangbei, the world's largest electronics market district, and the densest concentration of PCB, consumer electronics, and smart hardware factories on earth.

Find Shenzhen Suppliers

What Shenzhen Makes

Ranked by supply chain depth, supplier density, and export track record

Huaqiangbei: 1.45 km², 40+ markets, 20,000 merchants, 480B+ RMB in annual transactions

A significant share of the world’s consumer electronics pass through Shenzhen’s supply chain. Huaqiangbei in Futian district is the nerve centre: SEG Plaza alone has 3,000+ component shops across 10 floors. Bao’an district runs PCB assembly. Longhua hosts Foxconn’s largest global campus. Nanshan concentrates R&D for Huawei, ZTE, DJI, and Tencent.

You can source individual IC chips, resistors, and connectors from Huaqiangbei, or commission full PCBA runs from Bao’an factories with MOQs as low as 1 piece for prototypes. PCB prototypes cost $2 and arrive next day. 1688 search terms: 深圳电子元器件, 深圳PCB打样, 深圳PCBA加工. Sub-marketplace: ec.1688.com for electronics.

LED chips, displays, strips, stage lighting, drivers — Shenzhen makes the components that Zhongshan assembles

Shenzhen sits upstream in China’s lighting supply chain. Zhongshan (Guzhen) assembles finished fixtures, but Shenzhen factories in Bao’an and Longgang produce the LED chips, drivers, controllers, and display modules that go inside them. DJI’s hometown — 70%+ of global consumer drone production runs through this city

Motors, ESC controllers, carbon fibre frames, camera gimbals, flight controllers — every drone component is sourced within the city.

Consumer drones, FPV racing drones, agricultural drones, drone accessories, motors, ESC controllers. Budget consumer drones wholesale from $30. 1688 terms: 深圳无人机, 深圳FPV穿越机, 深圳电机, 深圳ESC电调. The CHTF (Nov 26-28) has a dedicated robotics section.

Shuibei handles 60% of China’s gold jewelry trade — gold at 24% below retail

Shuibei district in Luohu processes 60% of China’s gold jewelry and 70% of its diamond wholesale. Gold trades at 710 RMB/g vs 930 retail — 24% cheaper. The district has 9,000+ merchants across multiple buildings anchored by the Shuibei International Jewelry Trade Center. Metro Line 3/7 to Tianbei Station.

Gold, silver, diamonds, jade, pearls, gemstones, finished jewelry, loose stones, fashion jewelry. The Shuibei cluster includes Bolin International (50B RMB annual volume), Jinli International (high-end gold and diamond), and Jinzhan Plaza (silver and fashion pieces). 1688 terms: 深圳黄金首饰, 水贝珠宝批发. Note for 2026: Shuibei faces margin pressure after a 13.3 billion yuan gold shop collapse and a proposed 5% consumption tax. Processing fees have compressed to around 2%. Verify current conditions before committing to large orders.

Nanyou in Nanshan: 4,000+ merchants across 20 buildings, Asia’s largest original-design women’s fashion hub

Shenzhen is not a fashion city in the way Guangzhou is. But Nanyou wholesale market in Nanshan district has carved a niche in original-design women’s fashion. 4,000+ merchants across 20+ buildings, each floor with a different price tier and style. Buildings 107/108 (Tairon) carry young Chinese designer brands. Building 109 specialises in silk. Buildings 110/111 are the premium powerhouses.

Century Plaza sells factory-direct originals. Feima City (buildings 204/209) handles budget wholesale. ‘Higher floors = more surprises’ — factory showrooms on the top floors supply the stalls below at source pricing. Metro Line 9/12 to Nanyou Station, Exit F1. 1688 terms: 深圳南油女装, 深圳原创设计女装.

Mingtong Digital Mall draws 200,000 visitors daily — the world’s phone accessory wholesale centre

Huaqiangbei is where phone accessories are sourced at scale. Mingtong Digital Mall alone covers 70,000 m² with 60,000+ employees handling phone cases, screen protectors, cables, chargers, power banks, and Bluetooth accessories. Yuanwang Digital Mall next door handles ~60% of China’s wholesale refurbished iPhone distribution.

How to Find Suppliers in Shenzhen

Four ways to find Shenzhen suppliers. Most successful importers use multiple approaches at different stages.

Search on 1688

Shenzhen has the densest concentration of electronics manufacturers on 1688. Use the \u641C\u5DE5\u5382 (Search Factory) tab to filter verified manufacturers, and check 1688's electronics sub-marketplaces: ec.1688.com, 3c.1688.com, ee.1688.com, light.1688.com.

How to find Shenzhen suppliers on 1688

1
Search in Chinese

Use Chinese product names. Examples: 深圳TWS耳机 (TWS earphones), 深圳PCB打样 (PCB prototyping), 深圳充电宝 (power banks), 深圳智能家居 (smart home). WorldFirst’s AI Sourcing Agent translates for you.

2
Filter by Shenzhen

Set the supplier location filter to 深圳 (Shenzhen) or 广东 (Guangdong) province. Use 深圳产业带 (Shenzhen Industrial Belt) on industry.1688.com for curated manufacturers.

3
Check factory badges

Look for 实力商家 (verified merchant) and 源头工厂 (source factory) tags. For electronics, check if they list SMT production lines. Shenzhen has many hybrid companies — check 经营范围 for 生产 (production) vs 销售 (sales).

4
Verify before paying

Cross-check the business registration at gsxt.gov.cn. Bao’an, Longhua, or Longgang addresses = factories. Futian or Luohu = trading companies. Pay through 1688 World Pay for protection.
